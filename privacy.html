Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Purrap Packaging (“Purrap,” “we,” “us”) provides custom-printed restaurant packaging to businesses across New England. This policy explains what information we collect when you use purrappackaging.com and how we handle it.

Information we collect

When you submit our quote or contact form, we collect the details you provide — such as your name, email address, phone number, restaurant name, product interest, and any order details you choose to share. We use this information only to respond to your request, prepare a quote and proof, and fulfill your order.

How we use it

We use your information to reply to inquiries, send digital proofs and pricing, process and deliver orders, and keep you updated about your request. We do not sell your information. We do not share it except with service providers who help us operate (for example, our email delivery provider) or as required by law.

Data retention

We keep quote and order information for as long as needed to serve you and to meet our business and legal obligations, then delete or anonymize it.

Your choices

You may ask us to access, correct, or delete the information you’ve shared. Email manny.encarnacion@purrappackaging.com and we’ll take care of it.
